PUBG BATTELGROUND MOBILE

PUBG, short for PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole. In the game, players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to eliminate other players while avoiding being eliminated themselves. The last player or team standing wins the match.


M416

The M416 is a versatile assault rifle featured in PUBG, known for its balanced performance, manageable recoil, and adaptability to various combat situations. It's a popular choice among players for its reliability and effectiveness in both close and long-range engagements.
